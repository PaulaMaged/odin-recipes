Chickpea Salad

Description

A true love from first sight, I have stumbled upon this mouth running beast while seaarching for vegan meals. It has never left my taste buds since. It is creamy smooth and is bundled with enhancing flavours like garlic and paprica to make this one satiating snack, or heck even a full meal. I wouldn't mind

Ingrediants

  • chickpeas
  • lemons
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • papricka
  • tahini

steps

  1. boil chickpeas in water until it is soft and squishable
  2. put all ingrediants in blender
  3. add water until texture is just about smooth
  4. test taste until it suits your likings
  5. put in refrigator to coolen up for eating
